Term Naming Information

Stanford uses numeric codes to refer to each term. These numeric codes are the "short names" of each term for the waivers system. In short:

4-digit code:

  • First digit is a 1
  • Second two digits are the two-digit year when the school year ends (e.g.: the 2009-10 school year ends in 2010, so the two digits are "10"; the 2012-13 year ends in 2013, so "13")
  • The fourth digit is 2 for fall, 4 for winter, 6 for spring, and 8 for summer.
