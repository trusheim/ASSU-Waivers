Waivers for the Student Activities Fee assessed by the University on behalf of the Associated Students of Stanford University (ASSU) are available each academic quarter when fees are assessed (Fall, Winter, and Spring) between 8:00 AM on the first day of the quarter and 5:00 PM on the second Friday of the quarter.
