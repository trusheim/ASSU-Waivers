Waiver requests for the ASSU Student Activities Fee for {{ term }} are now open. Eligible members of the ASSU may request a waiver from any or all of the fees charged to them this quarter.

Please note:

  • Only currently enrolled undergraduate, graduate, and law students may request fee waivers.
  • Students on NCAA full-ride scholarships may not request fee waivers.

  • Waiver requests do not roll over to next quarter. You must submit a new fee waiver request every quarter, if desired.
  • Waivers will be posted to your University bill as a "Student Activities Credit" by the seventh week of the quarter.
